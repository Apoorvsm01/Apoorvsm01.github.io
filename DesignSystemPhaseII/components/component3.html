Usage:

Use cards to display content in a compact and visually appealing way. They can be used for various purposes, such as showcasing projects, displaying product information, or presenting articles.

Accessibility Considerations:

Ensure that the card content is accessible to all users, including those using screen readers. Use appropriate ARIA roles and attributes to enhance accessibility. Provide alternative text for images and ensure that interactive elements are keyboard-navigable.
