Usage:

Use the footer component at the bottom of your web pages to provide users with essential information and quick access to contact options. It should be consistent across all pages of your site.

Accessibility Considerations:

Ensure that the footer is keyboard navigable and that all links are accessible. Use appropriate ARIA roles if necessary, and ensure that color contrast meets accessibility standards.
