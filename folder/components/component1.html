Usage:

Use this navbar component to provide a consistent navigation experience across your website. It is designed to be responsive, adapting to different screen sizes.

Accessibility Considerations:

Ensure that the navbar is keyboard navigable and that all links have descriptive text. Use ARIA attributes where necessary to enhance screen reader support.
